Here's how it works.

There are no logins. Just post.

Hi there, and thanks for visiting! We originally pre-launched this site to a select audience at Hacker News, getting feedback on a work in progress. What we didn't expect is the amount of interest it would gather, particularly being tweeted by someone with 300,000 followers. Oops.

We'd like to be honest here and point out that the site is still a work in progress. That said, we add new features every day. We already have videos, the live feed, and interaction-based ranking. If you'd like a feature, just post it - we read each and every one. As for the bugs, we're working as hard as we can.

One thing I really appreciate about the early users is the smart, thoughtful and original content. Make it interesting: an insight into your mind, something that makes you think, or something beautiful. Cheers guys. Stay awesome.
