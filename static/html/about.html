Q. So what is this?

A. I'm Everyone is a live anonymous community. Each user on the site has the power to instantly communicate with hundreds of thousand people. There are no user accounts, and all posts are anonymous.

Q. What should I say?

A. Something about yourself that you don't any anyone else to judge.

Q. What images, video should I include?

Something describing how you feel. You can upload images directly by hitting the 'Pick file' button, or include a link to an image or video on Youtube, Flickr, Vimeo, or most other sites.

Q. What shouldn't I post?

Our content is determined by our host, Rackspace. Read their TOS here http://www.rackspacecloud.com/legal/aup. We actively filter for and remove certain types of content.

Q. I made a thoughtful, interesting post and the lameness filter deleted my stuff. What should I do?

Sorry! Sometimes our spam filters get a little aggressive. Try using different words or images. Better yet contact us, let us know what you were posting and we'll try and fix things.

Q. Where are the ads?

We insert ads into the content stream every few minutes. We keep them for stuff you'll find interesting.
